BACHELOR OF SCIENCE [BSc]

The Bachelor of Science is three-year program designed for students with a strong interest in Mathematics and Science.The course is considered as a foundation course for students who want to make their career in the field of Science.The Bachelor of science is an intense program leading to a double major that allows students to develop strong knowledge and skills in either discipline.

Admission Process:Direct Admission, based on the intermediate marks .Some top Colleges conduct counselling

Eligibility:10+2 qualifying degree from a recognized board.

Exam Type:Conduct semester wise exams.
