BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATION [BCA]

A Bachelor of Computer Applications is an undergraduate course that will build the knowledge of the students regarding computer language.By completing this three-year course,the students will be able to build their career in Information Technology and Computer Applications field.The typical syllabus of BCA includes database management systems,operating systems,software engineering,web technology such as C,C++,HTML,Java etc.

To pursue a BCA programme,one need not have physics,Chemistry and Maths as subjects in Class 12th.In fact,candidates who have pursued Arts or Commerce in class XII can also join the course.

Admission Process:Direct Admission, based on the intermediate marks .Some top Colleges conduct counselling

Eligibility:10+2 qualifying degree from a recognized board.

Exam Type:Conduct semester wise exams.
