Mahatma Gandhi Memorial College is arts, commerce and science college located in the town of Udupi, Karnataka, India. MGM college is run by the Academy of General Education, Manipal. The college also emphasizes on sports and other extra curricular activites. 
 FFounded in 1949, MGM is one of the largest and most respected colleges in Karnataka    .Ours is diverse learning community .  Each department have a strong focus on overall student development. All classroom teaching is supplemented with practical skills augmenting activities ,strengthening communication and confidence.
