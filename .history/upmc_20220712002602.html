UPENDRA PAI MEMORIAL COLLEGE   
  College in Udupi,Karnataka 
 
 Upendra Pai Memorial College(UPMC) is managed by Konkani Liguistic Minority Trust and situated in city Udupi state of Karnataka.The college offers two courses Bachelor of Commerce[B.Com] and Bachelor of Business Management[BBM].The college is affiliated by Mangalore University and Accredition Council(NAAC).
The  main aim of college is to provide excellence in Management Education.The college conducts courses on Entrepreneurship Development and Individual Development for the final year degree students in order to make them better equipped to face the problem of life.
