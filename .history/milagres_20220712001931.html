MILAGRES COLLEGE,KALLIANPUR

 

  College in Kallianpur, Karnataka 

 

Milagres College is a college in Kallianpur,Udupi,Karnataka.The college offers four courses Bachelor of Commerce[B.Com],Bachelor of Arts[BA] ,Bachelor of Science[B.Sc]   and Bachelor of Business Management[BBM].The college is affiliated by Mangalore University.
