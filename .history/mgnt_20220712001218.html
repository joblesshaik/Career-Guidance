BACHELOR OF BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION[BBA]

Bachelor of Business Management(BBM) is an undergraduate course related to the fields of management and commerce.Generally,the duration of the BBM Course is 3 years,which  is divided into semesters of study.The course focuses on the study of subjects related to the management of business and organization and also on the development of entrepreneurial skills of a candidate.The course sharpens the analytical and critical thinking skills of a student by training them in the practical as well as theoretical aspects of business and business management. 

Admission Process:Direct Admission, based on the intermediate marks .Some top Colleges conduct counselling

Eligibility:10+2 qualifying degree from a recognized board.

Exam Type:Conduct semester wise exams.
